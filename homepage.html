About


Set amidst lush greenery, rolling hills and majestic mountains, The Bala's Holiday Chalet offers you good value for money. A charming English style inn that provides the perfect blend of old world ambience and contemporary comfort. With its serenity and country charm, the hotel captures the very essence of a highland holiday.

Situated on one of Malaysia's most pristine natural forest in the Cameron Highlands, the cool air combined with unspoilt beauty amidst natural surroundings makes it a perfect holiday destination for those interested in a highland holiday.

The ideal location, provides stunning views across the mountains and the picturesque gardens provide a perfect setting to a dream holiday.

Facilities


  • Former British school, fully refurbished to capture its original history and character
  • Tastefully renovated cottage style rooms overlooking the forest and surrounding picturesque garden.
  • All rooms with wireless broadband facility (WIFI)
  • Quiet and peaceful with some rooms capturing the sunrise and sunset and yet some others with their own fireplace
  • Cozy reading room and library with a fireplace
  • The most talked about tea & scones in the highlands is at the Planters Tea Room at The Bala's Holiday Chalet, serving mouth watering afternoon tea & scones with homemade strawberry jam and cream
  • British India Restaurant serving home cooked delicious curries, western cuisine and traditional Malaysian dishes
  • Recreational activities arranged by the hotel include daily Countryside Tours, Adventure Tours, Tea Plantation Tours, Gunung Brinchang Tour and Guided Jungle Trekking. Others include an Orang Asli Experience.
  • Laundry Facility.
  • Bus ticketing / Taxi transfers and Safe Deposit facilities
  • Internet Access / Fax / ISD / STD telephone facilities.
  • Free shuttle service to/from Tanah Rata town. (Refer to schedule on notice board. All other times transfers are available with a surcharge)
  • Long stay discounts available.
  • Wedding packages available - Garden or Indoor setting with deco and food arrangements. Celebrate the magic of your love with our wedding packages tailored to suite your budget. Packages are available from RM12,500 nett for 100 guests. Menu options are not included in this rate. Kindly enquire at reception.
  • Wedding Photography (Front and Back Garden only) also available. Minimum RM250 p/hour. Our picturesque setting has become a popular backdrop for matrimonial bliss and memories.
